# Net Revenue Management Analysis

End-to-end Excel case study focused on **net revenue management (NRM)** for the shampoo category.  
This project combines market data, internal sales data, profitability analysis, promotion assessment, and 2024 growth scenarios to support commercial decision-making.

## Overview

The workbook analyzes how a shampoo business can grow revenue and profit by answering questions such as:

- Which suppliers and brands are gaining or losing share?
- Which subcategories are growing fastest?
- Which SKUs contribute the most to net sales and gross profit?
- What is the impact of promotions on sales uplift?
- What is the expected 2024 outlook, including innovation and pack-size opportunities?

## Analytical Workflow

### 1. Market and brand performance
The workbook uses external market data to evaluate:

- annual value sales trends
- supplier and brand share evolution
- market share by year
- category and subcategory dynamics

### 2. Internal profitability
Internal SKU-level data is used to calculate:

- net sales
- gross profit per unit
- gross profit per SKU
- gross margin
- net sales contribution

### 3. Opportunity assessment
The model explores growth levers such as:

- entering faster-growing subcategories
- launching an **organic shampoo**
- adding a **50ml pack-size**
- improving promotional effectiveness
- forecasting 2024 net sales

## Key Insights

Based on the workbook structure and the underlying data reviewed:

- **Organic** is the fastest-growing subcategory in the dataset, making it a strong innovation candidate.
- In **2023**, **Starbust** appears to be the largest brand by value share, followed by **Harmonix**, **Vitalize**, and **Shinez**.
- The most important internal SKUs by net sales contribution include:
  - **Shinez Repair 100ml**
  - **Starbust Ultra Soft 100ml**
  - **Starbust Ultra Soft 150ml**
- High-contribution SKUs also show strong gross profit generation, which makes them priority products for pricing, promotion, and assortment decisions.
- The workbook links commercial actions to a **waterfall view** of estimated 2024 net sales.
